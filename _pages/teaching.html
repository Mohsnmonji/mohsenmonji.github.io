--- layout: archive title: "Teaching" permalink: /teaching/ author_profile: true --- ## **Teaching Philosophy** I approach teaching as an encouraging and enjoyable learning experience where students feel supported in developing their skills. I teach **Sociology of Health**, **Quantitative Research Methods**, and **Applied Social Statistics**, with an emphasis on connecting course material to real-world applications. In my courses, I incorporate **experiential learning** to provide students with more opportunities to work with real-world data to examine a wide range of topics, such as income inequality, food insecurity, housing, and disparities in health outcomes. I believe this hands-on approach not only helps students build technical expertise but also gives them a chance to see the impact that their learning can have in the world. I value slow and thoughtful learning and encourage students to take the time to engage with the concepts and methods they are exposed to in the course. My goal is to create a learning environment where students feel confident and inspired to learn. --- ## **Teaching Experience** ### Lecturer - **Social Statistics**, Department of Sociology and Anthropology, Concordia University (Winter 2025) Designed a course titled **Statistics for Social Good**, integrating experiential learning to teach statistics in engaging ways for exploring social inequalities like income, food security, and housing in Canada. ### Teaching Assistant & Tutorial Leader - **Statistics II**, Department of Sociology and Anthropology, Concordia University (Winter 2024, Fall 2023 - 3 classes) - **Statistics I**, Department of Sociology and Anthropology, Concordia University (Fall 2025) - **Research Methods**, Department of Sociology and Anthropology, Concordia University (Winter 2022) - **Quantitative Research Methods for Practitioners**, Department of Applied Human Science, Concordia University (Winter 2022) - **Classical Social Theory**, Department of Sociology and Anthropology, Concordia University (Winter 2025, Fall 2024, Winter 2023, Fall 2022, Winter 2022, Fall 2021) ### Tutor - **Social Statistics and Quantitative Research Methods**, Tehran, Iran (2016â€“2020) Taught quantitative research methods and social statistics to undergraduate students at the University of Tehran. ---
